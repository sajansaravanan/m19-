NASA's Webb Captures the Iconic Horsehead Nebula

The James Webb Space Telescope has captured the top of the iconic Horsehead Nebula in unprecedented detail, showcasing the intricate structures within this famous cosmic feature.

Key Highlights

The Horsehead Nebula, located in the constellation Orion, is a region of active star formation.

Webb's infrared capabilities reveal hidden details obscured by dust and gas in the nebula.

This observation provides crucial insights into the processes of star formation and the life cycle of stars.
